Part base
CompositePart base

sensor/
IMU: gets noised angular rate + noised acceleration + gravity
DVL: gets noised velocity measurement
GPS: gets a noised LLA (lat long alt) measurement using geodesy in earth frame (postpone until Earth field/planets are sorted out)
Mag: gets a noised magnetometer reading (requires MagField), sort the details out later.

actuator/
Thruster: applies a commanded force along the z axis (can be negative or positive)
PropThruster: like a thruster but also has a counter-torque coefficient
Motor: CompositePart that rotates its children about its Z axis (see plan.md for more info on articulated parts)
GimbalTwoAxis: CompositePart that rotates its children in 2 axis, same functionality as two nested orthogonal motors. 

structural/
PointMass: has a given mass and no moi
Mass: has a given mass and moi
PointBuoy: has a given volume and computes buoyant force by querying density
Surface: gets the local-frame fluid velocity and multiplies it by a tensor to produce force in and another tensor to get torque. 
Surface<N>: like Surface but now has N tensors for both force and torque where the Nth tensor is multiplied by v^N. So for example Surface<2> would compute A @ v + B @ v^2. Actually makes normal Surface part redundant so it can be replaced by Surface<1>

coupling/
TetherEndpoint: attaches two Crafts
